# Shopping Cart & Checkout Process

Learn how to review your cart and complete your purchase securely through the checkout process.

## Shopping Cart Overview

Your shopping cart stores selected products before checkout. The cart is accessible from anywhere on the site.

### Accessing Your Cart

**Method 1: Cart Sidebar** (Quick View)
- Click the shopping cart icon in the header
- A sidebar opens from the right showing your cart contents
- Make quick edits without leaving your current page

**Method 2: Full Cart Page** (`/cart`)
- Navigate to the dedicated cart page for a full-screen view
- Ideal for reviewing larger orders

### Cart Display

Your cart shows:
- **Cart Icon Badge**: Number of items in your cart (visible in header)
- **Product List**: All items with images, names, and prices
- **Quantity Controls**: Adjust quantities for each item
- **Unit Price**: Price per individual item
- **Subtotal**: Price × quantity for each item
- **Cart Total**: Total amount for all items
- **Item Count**: Total number of products in cart

<Note>
  The cart is stored in your browser's local storage. Your items will persist across sessions until you clear them or complete a purchase.
</Note>

## Managing Cart Items

### Updating Quantities

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate Product">
    Find the item you want to update in your cart
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Adjust Quantity">
    Use the quantity controls:
    - Click **+** to increase quantity
    - Click **-** to decrease quantity
    - Minimum quantity is 1
    - Maximum is limited by available stock
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="See Updated Total">
    The subtotal and cart total update automatically
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Removing Items

**Remove Single Item:**
- Click the **"Quitar"** (Remove) button or trash icon next to the item
- The item is immediately removed from your cart
- Totals update automatically

**Clear Entire Cart:**
- Click **"Vaciar carrito"** (Empty cart) at the bottom of the cart
- All items are removed at once
- Confirmation is immediate

<Warning>
  Removing items cannot be undone. You'll need to add them back to your cart if removed accidentally.
</Warning>

## Proceeding to Checkout

When you're ready to complete your purchase:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Cart">
    Ensure all items and quantities are correct in your cart
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Checkout">
    Click the **"Ir a pagar"** (Go to checkout) button in the cart sidebar or cart page
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to Checkout">
    You'll be redirected to `/checkout` to complete your purchase
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The checkout button is disabled if your cart is empty.
</Note>

## Checkout Page

The checkout page (`/checkout`) is where you provide shipping information and complete payment.

### Checkout Page Layout

The page is divided into two main sections:

**Left Column: Information Forms** (Steps 1-2)
- Contact Information
- Shipping Address

**Right Column: Order Summary**
- Product list with prices
- Subtotal
- Shipping cost
- Total amount
- Payment button

### Step 1: Contact Information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Full Name">
    Enter your complete name (first and last name required)
    
    **Validation:**
    - Must contain only letters and spaces
    - Must include at least 2 words (first and last name)
    - Special characters are automatically filtered
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Email Address">
    Provide a valid email address for order confirmation
    
    **Validation:**
    - Must be a valid email format (e.g., user@example.com)
    - Used to send order confirmation and updates
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Phone Number">
    Enter your Chilean phone number
    
    **Format:** +56 9 XXXX XXXX
    - Automatically formatted as you type
    - Must be a valid Chilean mobile number (+569 followed by 8 digits)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  All contact fields are validated in real-time. Error messages appear below each field if the input is invalid.
</Tip>

### Step 2: Shipping Address

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete Address">
    Enter your full delivery address
    
    **Required Information:**
    - Street name and number
    - Apartment/unit number (if applicable)
    - Comuna (district)
    - City
    
    **Minimum Length:** 10 characters for specificity
    
    **Example:** Calle Example 123, Depto 4B, Comuna, Ciudad
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Additional Notes (Optional)">
    Add special instructions for your order:
    - Preferred color options
    - Delivery instructions
    - Customization requests
    - Any other relevant information
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Order Summary (Right Column)

**Product List:**
Shows each item with:
- Product title
- Quantity
- Unit price
- Subtotal per item

**Cost Breakdown:**
```
Subtotal:    CLP XX,XXX
Envío:       CLP 3,500
─────────────────────
Total:       CLP XX,XXX
```

<Note>
  **Standard Shipping Cost**: CLP 3,500 is added to all orders
</Note>

### Form Validation

The checkout form validates all fields before allowing payment:

**Real-time Validation:**
- Fields highlight in red if invalid
- Error messages appear below problematic fields
- Specific guidance provided for each error

**Common Validation Errors:**
- "El nombre solo debe contener letras y espacios" - Name contains invalid characters
- "Ingresa nombre y apellido" - Only one name provided
- "Ingresa un correo electrónico válido" - Invalid email format
- "Ingresa un número chileno válido" - Phone number format incorrect
- "La dirección debe ser más específica" - Address too short

**Submission Requirements:**
- All required fields must be filled
- All fields must pass validation
- No validation errors present

The **"Pagar con Flow"** button is disabled until all requirements are met.

## Payment Process

Black Michi Studio uses Flow for secure payment processing.

### Initiating Payment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete Form">
    Ensure all checkout fields are correctly filled and validated
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Order">
    Double-check your order summary:
    - Products and quantities
    - Total amount
    - Shipping address
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Payment Button">
    Click **"Pagar con Flow"** (Pay with Flow)
    
    The button shows a loading spinner with "Procesando..." while creating the payment
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Order Creation">
    The system:
    - Creates a new order (pedido) with status **"pendiente"** (pending)
    - Validates product availability and stock
    - Generates a unique order ID (commerce_order)
    - Links to your account if you're logged in
    - Stores all order details
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Redirect to Flow">
    You're automatically redirected to Flow's secure payment gateway
    
    A pending order record is saved to localStorage for tracking
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  **Do not close the browser** during payment processing or you may lose your order reference.
</Warning>

### Flow Payment Gateway

On the Flow payment page:

1. **Review Order**: See your order details and total amount
2. **Choose Payment Method**: Select from available payment options (credit card, debit card, etc.)
3. **Enter Payment Details**: Provide your payment information securely
4. **Confirm Payment**: Complete the transaction

<Note>
  Flow handles all payment processing securely. Black Michi Studio never stores your payment card details.
</Note>

### Payment Status Codes

Flow returns one of four statuses:

| Status Code | Internal Status | Description |
|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| 1 | `pendiente` | Payment is being processed |
| 2 | `pagado` | Payment successful |
| 3 | `rechazado` | Payment rejected |
| 4 | `cancelado` | Payment cancelled by user |

## After Payment

### Return to Store

After completing or cancelling payment, Flow redirects you back to the store at `/payment/return`.

### Payment Result Page

The payment result page displays different screens based on the outcome:

#### ✅ Payment Successful (`estado: pagado`)

**You'll see:**
- Green success message: "¡Pago exitoso!"
- Your order number (Pedido #XX)
- Confirmation that an email was sent to your registered address
- **"Volver a la tienda"** (Return to store) button

**What Happens:**
- Your cart is automatically cleared
- Order status changes to **"pagado"**
- Stock is deducted for purchased products
- Payment details are recorded
- `fecha_pago` (payment date) is set
- Order confirmation email is sent

<Success>
  Your order is confirmed! You'll receive tracking information via email once it ships.
</Success>

#### ⏳ Payment Pending (`estado: pendiente`)

**You'll see:**
- Yellow pending message: "Pago pendiente"
- Information that payment is being processed
- Note that you'll be notified by email
- **"Volver a la tienda"** button

**What This Means:**
- Payment is still processing
- Order remains in pending status
- You'll receive email notification once confirmed

#### ❌ Payment Rejected (`estado: rechazado`)

**You'll see:**
- Red error message: "Pago rechazado"
- Explanation that payment couldn't be processed
- **"Reintentar"** (Retry) button to try payment again
- **"Volver"** (Go back) button to return home

**What to Do:**
- Verify your payment details
- Check with your bank if needed
- Try payment again or use a different method
- Your cart items remain available

#### ⚠️ Payment Error

**You'll see:**
- Red error message: "Error en el pago"
- General error information
- **"Reintentar"** button

**Possible Causes:**
- Connection issues
- Server error
- Invalid payment token
- Missing order information

<Warning>
  If you encounter repeated payment errors, please contact customer support with your order ID.
</Warning>

## Stock Management During Checkout

The system validates stock at multiple points:

### Pre-Payment Validation

Before creating the Flow payment:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Product Availability Check">
    System verifies all products in cart still exist and are active
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Stock Verification">
    Confirms sufficient stock for requested quantities
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Error Handling">
    If validation fails:
    - Transaction is rolled back
    - Error message displays which products are unavailable
    - You can remove unavailable items and try again
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Post-Payment Stock Deduction

After successful payment (status = 2):

- Stock is automatically deducted from products
- Each product's stock count is reduced by the ordered quantity
- `updated_at` timestamp is updated
- If stock becomes insufficient after payment (rare edge case), a warning is logged

<Note>
  Stock is reserved only after confirmed payment, not at cart addition. This ensures accurate inventory and prevents overselling.
</Note>

## Order Confirmation Email

After successful payment, you'll receive an email containing:

- Order number and confirmation
- Items purchased with quantities and prices
- Shipping address
- Total amount paid
- Estimated delivery timeframe (3-5 días)
- Contact information for support

<Tip>
  **Didn't receive your email?** Check your spam folder or contact support with your order number.
</Tip>

## Guest vs Registered Checkout

### Guest Checkout
- No account required
- Enter contact information each time
- Order email sent to provided address
- Limited order tracking (via email links)

### Registered User Checkout
- Pre-filled contact information
- Saved shipping addresses
- Order history in account dashboard
- Easy reordering of past purchases
- Order tracking via account panel

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### "Stock insuficiente" Error

**Problem:** Not enough stock for your requested quantity

**Solution:**
- Reduce quantity for affected items
- Remove out-of-stock items from cart
- Try again with updated quantities

### Payment Not Processing

**Problem:** Payment appears stuck or doesn't redirect

**Solutions:**
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure pop-ups are not blocked
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Contact support if issue persists

### Email Not Received

**Problem:** No confirmation email after successful payment

**Solutions:**
- Wait 10-15 minutes (emails may be delayed)
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email address was entered correctly
- Contact support with order number

### Validation Errors

**Problem:** Cannot proceed due to form errors

**Solutions:**
- Read error messages carefully
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check format requirements (email, phone)
- Clear field and re-enter if validation seems stuck
