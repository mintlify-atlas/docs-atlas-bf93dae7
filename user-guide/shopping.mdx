# Shopping & Browsing Products

Discover how to explore and find the perfect 3D printed products in the Black Michi Studio catalog.

## Browsing the Home Page

When you first visit the store, you'll see:

- **Hero Section**: Featured promotions and hero images showcasing our latest offerings
- **Category Cards**: Quick access to product categories like Vasos 3D, Placas Navi, and Figuras
- **Popular Products**: A curated selection of trending items
- **Secondary Hero Section**: Additional featured content

## Navigating by Category

### Desktop Navigation

The header includes a category navigation bar:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Category Menu">
    Click the **"COMPRAR POR CATEGORÍA"** button in the navigation bar. This opens a sidebar with all available categories.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Categories">
    The sidebar displays:
    - **Todos los Productos** - View all products
    - **Vasos 3D** - Personalized 3D cups
    - **Placas Navi** - Decorative Navi plaques
    - **Figuras** - Collectible 3D figures
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select a Category">
    Click on any category name to filter products. The page will navigate to `/productos?categoria={categoryId}` showing only items from that category.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Quick Category Access

Categories are also displayed directly in the header navigation bar for quick access. Simply click any category name to filter products instantly.

<Note>
  The navigation bar is scrollable on smaller screens to accommodate all categories.
</Note>

## Product List Page

The product list (`/productos`) displays products in a responsive grid layout:

### Product Cards

Each product card shows:
- **Product Image**: Hover to see alternate images (if available)
- **Product Title**: Clear name of the item
- **Star Rating**: Average customer rating (1-5 stars)
- **Price**: Current price in CLP (Chilean Pesos)
- **Add to Cart Button**: Quick add functionality (disabled if out of stock)

### Filtering & Sorting

The product list supports several filtering options:

- **By Category**: Filter by selecting a category from the navigation
- **By Search**: Use the search bar to find products by title or description
- **By Price**: Filter products by minimum price
- **By Rating**: Show only products with a specific star rating or higher

**Sort Options:**
- Newest first (default)
- Price: Low to High
- Price: High to Low
- Name (A-Z)

### Pagination

Products are displayed 12 per page. Use the pagination controls at the bottom:
- **Previous/Next Arrows**: Navigate between pages
- **Page Number**: Shows current page number

<Tip>
  The pagination controls only appear when there are more than 12 products to display.
</Tip>

## Search Functionality

To search for products:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Search">
    Products can be filtered using the search parameter `?busqueda=query`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Search Results">
    The search looks through:
    - Product titles
    - Product descriptions
    
    Results update automatically to show matching products.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Product Details

Click on any product card to view its detailed page (`/producto/{productId}`).

### Product Detail Page Features

**Image Gallery:**
- Main product image with zoom functionality (hover to zoom in)
- Thumbnail navigation for multiple images
- Arrow controls to cycle through images
- Image counter showing position (e.g., "1 / 3")

**Product Information:**
- Category badge
- Product title
- Average rating with review count
- Stock availability indicator
- Full product description
- Current price (with previous price if discounted)
- Discount percentage badge (if applicable)

**Product Benefits:**
- 📦 Envío en 3-5 días (Delivery in 3-5 days)
- 🛡️ Garantía 30 días (30-day warranty)

**Tabs Section:**
- **Descripción**: Detailed product description and features
- **Especificaciones**: Technical specifications (material, dimensions, print time)
- **Reseñas**: Customer reviews and ratings

<Warning>
  You must be logged in to view and submit product reviews.
</Warning>

## Adding Products to Cart

### From Product List

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Your Product">
    Browse or search for the product you want
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Add to Cart">
    Click the **"Agregar al carrito"** button on the product card
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    The product is added with a quantity of 1. The cart icon in the header updates to show the item count.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### From Product Detail Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Quantity">
    Use the quantity selector (+ and - buttons) to choose how many items you want. The maximum is limited by available stock.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add to Cart">
    Click the **"Agregar al carrito"** button. The specified quantity will be added to your cart.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Visual Feedback">
    The button briefly changes color to confirm the action, and the cart count in the header updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Stock Checking**: The system validates stock availability. If a product is out of stock, the "Add to Cart" button will be disabled and show "Sin stock" (Out of stock).
</Note>

## Viewing Your Cart

### Cart Sidebar

Click the shopping cart icon in the header to open the cart sidebar:

**Cart Sidebar Features:**
- List of all items in your cart
- Product image and title
- Unit price and quantity
- Quantity controls (+ / - buttons)
- Remove item button (trash icon)
- Subtotal per item
- Cart total
- **"Ir a pagar"** (Go to checkout) button
- **"Vaciar carrito"** (Empty cart) link

### Updating Cart Items

- **Increase Quantity**: Click the + button (limited by stock)
- **Decrease Quantity**: Click the - button (minimum 1)
- **Remove Item**: Click the trash icon
- **Clear All**: Click "Vaciar carrito" at the bottom

<Tip>
  Your cart is stored in your browser's local storage, so items persist even if you refresh the page or return later.
</Tip>

## Stock Management

The platform actively tracks inventory:

- **In Stock**: Products show "✓ En stock" and are available for purchase
- **Low Stock**: The available quantity is displayed (e.g., "5 disponibles")
- **Out of Stock**: Products show "✗ Agotado" and cannot be added to cart
- **Cart Validation**: When checking out, the system verifies stock is still available

## Product Reviews

### Viewing Reviews

On the product detail page, click the **"Reseñas"** tab to see:
- Customer ratings (1-5 stars)
- Written reviews with reviewer name and date
- Average rating calculated across all reviews

### Writing a Review

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login Required">
    You must be logged in to write reviews. If not logged in, you'll see a prompt to log in.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Review Form">
    Go to the "Reseñas" tab on a product page
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Rate the Product">
    Click on the stars to select your rating (1-5 stars)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Write Your Review">
    Enter your comments in the text area, sharing your experience with the product
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **"Publicar reseña"** (Publish review) to submit. Your review will appear in the reviews list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Related Products

At the bottom of each product detail page, you'll find:
- **Productos relacionados**: Products from the same category
- **"Ver todos →"** link: View all products in that category

<Tip>
  Use related products to discover similar items and compare options before making your purchase.
</Tip>
