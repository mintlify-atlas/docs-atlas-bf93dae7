# Account Management

Create and manage your account to enjoy a personalized shopping experience with saved information, order history, and faster checkout.

## Benefits of Creating an Account

**With a Black Michi Studio account, you can:**

- 🚀 **Faster Checkout** - Save your contact information and shipping addresses
- 📦 **Order History** - View all your past orders in one place
- 🔄 **Easy Reordering** - Quickly reorder products you've purchased before
- ⭐ **Product Reviews** - Leave reviews and ratings for products
- 👤 **Profile Management** - Update your information anytime
- 📧 **Order Updates** - Receive email notifications about your orders
- 🎯 **Personalized Experience** - Get recommendations based on your purchases

<Note>
  You can also checkout as a guest without creating an account, but you'll miss out on these convenient features.
</Note>

## User Registration

Create your account to start enjoying member benefits.

### Accessing the Registration Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Register">
    Click **"Registro"** (Register) in the header navigation, or visit `/register`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Registration Form">
    The registration page displays the Black Michi Studio branding with a clean, modern form
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Registration Form Fields

Complete the following information:

#### 1. Full Name (Nombre completo)

**Requirements:**
- Your complete name (first and last name)
- Minimum 2 characters
- Only letters and spaces allowed

**Example:** Juan Pérez González

#### 2. Email Address (Correo electrónico)

**Requirements:**
- Valid email format (user@domain.com)
- Must be unique (not already registered)
- Used for login and order notifications

**Example:** juan.perez@ejemplo.com

#### 3. Password (Contraseña)

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 6 characters
- Can include letters, numbers, and special characters
- Protected with show/hide toggle (eye icon)

<Tip>
  Choose a strong password with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols for better security.
</Tip>

#### 4. Terms and Conditions

**Required:**
- Check the box to accept Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
- Registration cannot proceed without accepting terms

### Completing Registration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Fill Form">
    Enter all required information in the registration form
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Validation">
    Real-time validation checks each field:
    - Name validation (letters only, minimum length)
    - Email format validation
    - Password length validation
    - Terms acceptance requirement
    
    Error messages appear below fields if validation fails
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Registration">
    Click **"REGISTRARME"** (Register Me) button
    
    The button shows a loading spinner: "Registrando..." while processing
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Success Confirmation">
    On successful registration:
    - Green success message appears: "¡Registro exitoso! Redirigiendo..."
    - You're automatically logged in
    - Redirected to home page after 2 seconds
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Registration Process (Backend)

When you register, the system:

1. **Validates Input**: Checks all fields meet requirements
2. **Email Uniqueness**: Verifies email isn't already registered
3. **Password Hashing**: Securely encrypts your password using bcrypt
4. **Creates User Record**: Stores in `usuarios` table with:
   - User ID (auto-generated)
   - Name
   - Email (unique)
   - Hashed password
   - Role: `cliente` (client) by default
   - Active status: `true`
   - Created timestamp
   - Created IP address (for security)
5. **Auto-Login**: Generates authentication token
6. **Welcome Email**: Sends confirmation (if configured)

<Success>
  Your account is now active and ready to use!
</Success>

## User Login

Access your account to view orders and manage your profile.

### Accessing the Login Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Login">
    Click **"Iniciar Sesión"** (Login) in the header, or visit `/login`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Login Form">
    The login page displays with input fields for your credentials
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Login Form Fields

#### Email Address
- Enter the email you registered with
- Must match an existing account

#### Password
- Enter your account password
- Use the eye icon to show/hide password
- Case-sensitive

### Logging In

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Type your email and password in the respective fields
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Remember Me (Optional)">
    Check **"Recordarme"** (Remember Me) to stay logged in on this device
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **"Iniciar sesión"** (Login) button
    
    The button shows "Ingresando..." while authenticating
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Successful Login">
    After successful authentication:
    - Auth token is saved to localStorage
    - User info is stored in the session
    - You're redirected based on role:
      - **Admin users** → `/admin` dashboard
      - **Client users** → `/` home page
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  **Forgot Your Password?** Click "¿Olvidaste tu contraseña?" (Forgot password?) to reset it.
</Warning>

### Login Error Messages

- **"El correo es obligatorio"** - Email field is empty
- **"La contraseña es obligatoria"** - Password field is empty  
- **"Credenciales inválidas o error de servidor"** - Wrong email/password or server issue
- **"Error inesperado de servidor"** - Connection or server error

## User Profile Management

Manage your account information after logging in.

### Accessing Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open User Menu">
    Click on your name in the header navigation
    
    A dropdown menu appears with your account options
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Profile">
    **For Client Users:**
    - Click **"Mi Panel"** (My Panel) to access `/client`
    
    **For Admin Users:**
    - Click **"Panel Admin"** to access `/admin`
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Viewing Profile Information

The client panel (`/api/client/perfil`) displays your account details:

- **ID**: Your unique user ID
- **Name** (nombre): Your full name
- **Email**: Your registered email address
- **Role** (rol): Account type (`cliente` or `admin`)
- **Phone** (telefono): Contact phone number
- **Address** (direccion): Saved shipping address
- **Created At**: Account creation date

### Updating Profile Information

To update your profile:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Edit Mode">
    Navigate to your profile/panel page
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Editable Fields">
    You can update:
    - **Name** (nombre): Full name
    - **Phone** (telefono): Contact number  
    - **Address** (direccion): Default shipping address
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **"Actualizar Perfil"** or similar save button
    
    The system sends a PUT request to `/api/client/perfil`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Updated information is saved and displayed
    
    The `updated_at` timestamp is automatically updated
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Email cannot be changed** after registration for security reasons. Contact support if you need to update your email.
</Note>

### Profile Update (Backend)

When updating your profile:

1. **Authentication Check**: Verifies you're logged in
2. **Input Validation**: Validates the updated fields
3. **Database Update**: Updates `usuarios` table
4. **Timestamp**: Sets `updated_at` to current time
5. **Returns Updated Data**: Confirms changes with current profile info

## User Roles

Black Michi Studio has two user roles:

### Cliente (Client) - Standard User

**Permissions:**
- Browse and purchase products
- View own order history
- Update own profile
- Leave product reviews
- Access client panel (`/client`)

**Database:** `rol = 'cliente'` (default for new registrations)

### Admin - Administrator

**Permissions:**
- All client permissions plus:
- Access admin dashboard (`/admin`)
- Manage products (create, edit, delete)
- Manage categories
- View all orders from all customers
- Update order statuses
- Manage featured products
- Manage hero images
- View sales analytics

**Database:** `rol = 'admin'` (assigned manually in database)

<Warning>
  Admin role cannot be self-assigned and must be set in the database by a system administrator.
</Warning>

## Account Security

### Password Security

**Best Practices:**
- Use at least 6 characters (more is better)
- Combine uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Don't reuse passwords from other sites
- Never share your password

**System Security:**
- Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (10 rounds)
- Original passwords never stored in plain text
- Secure authentication with JWT tokens

### Account Protection

**Automatic Security Features:**
- Session tokens expire after period of inactivity
- IP address logged at registration for fraud detection
- Failed login attempts can be monitored
- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission

**Manual Security Steps:**
- Log out on shared computers
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable "Remember Me" only on personal devices
- Report suspicious activity immediately

## Account Status

Your account has an active status:

**Database Field:** `activo` (boolean)
- **true** - Account is active and can be used (default)
- **false** - Account is deactivated

**Deactivated Accounts:**
- Cannot log in
- Cannot place orders  
- Previous orders remain visible to admins
- Can be reactivated by administrators

<Tip>
  If you need to temporarily disable your account, contact customer support.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting Account Issues

### Can't Log In

**Problem:** Login fails with valid credentials

**Solutions:**
- Verify email is spelled correctly (case-insensitive)
- Check password (case-sensitive)
- Ensure Caps Lock is off
- Try password reset if forgotten
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try different browser

### Registration Email Already Exists

**Problem:** "Email already registered" error

**Solutions:**
- Use login instead of registration
- Try password reset if you forgot password
- Use different email address
- Contact support if you believe it's an error

### Not Receiving Emails

**Problem:** No confirmation or notification emails

**Solutions:**
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email address in profile is correct
- Add noreply@blackmichiestudio.com to contacts
- Wait 15-30 minutes for delivery
- Contact support if issue persists

### Profile Won't Update

**Problem:** Changes not saving to profile

**Solutions:**
- Ensure you're logged in
- Check validation error messages
- Verify all required fields are filled
- Try refreshing page and editing again
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support if issue continues
